About us

We believe having breakfast or to spend time over coffee with cats is very relaxing. Petting and listening to the cats or other animals is a real stress reliever.

Cat cafes originated in Taiwan, with one opening in Taipei in 1998. The cafe became popular with Japanese tourists as well as local visitors, who enjoyed the opportunity to interact with some furry friends. This new type of café is an opportunity for all animal lovers to spend time in the company of loving pets eager for cuddles, even if it is not possible to host one in their own house.

We want you to feel relieve after a long stressing day at work so drop by to Catfè.
